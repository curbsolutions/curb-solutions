# C.U.R.B. Solutions — Design Brainstorm

## Approach A: Marble Opulence
- **Design Movement**: Art Deco Luxury / Editorial Minimalism
- **Core Principles**: White marble texture as primary surface, deep forest green as dominant accent, gold as the luxury thread, generous whitespace for breathing room
- **Color Philosophy**: White (#F8F6F2 marble-tinted) + Deep Forest Green (#1A4731) + Antique Gold (#C9A84C) — evoking cleanliness, prestige, and nature
- **Layout Paradigm**: Full-bleed hero with diagonal gold divider, asymmetric two-column sections, left-anchored text with right-side imagery
- **Signature Elements**: Thin gold horizontal rules, marble texture panels, serif display headlines with italic script accents
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Subtle hover lifts on cards, gold underline reveals on links, smooth section fade-ins
- **Animation**: Fade-up on scroll, gold shimmer on CTA hover, staggered entrance for service cards
- **Typography System**: Playfair Display (serif, bold) for headings + Cormorant Garamond (italic) for taglines + Lato (sans) for body
- **Probability**: 0.08

## Approach B: Dark Luxury Noir
- **Design Movement**: Dark Luxury / High-End Service Branding
- **Core Principles**: Near-black background with marble vein overlays, gold as the only color, minimal copy, maximum impact
- **Color Philosophy**: Charcoal (#1C1C1C) + Warm Gold (#D4AF37) + Off-White (#F5F0E8) — premium night-time elegance
- **Layout Paradigm**: Full-screen sections with centered editorial layout, large display type, sparse content
- **Signature Elements**: Gold border frames, large quote typography, dark marble texture
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Gold glow on hover, parallax marble backgrounds, cinematic scroll
- **Animation**: Slow parallax, gold particle shimmer, dramatic fade-in
- **Typography System**: Bodoni Moda (display) + Raleway (body)
- **Probability**: 0.07

## Approach C: Emerald & Ivory Prestige (SELECTED)
- **Design Movement**: Contemporary Luxury Service / Premium Residential Brand
- **Core Principles**: Ivory/marble white as canvas, emerald green as brand anchor, gold as accent thread, clean editorial spacing
- **Color Philosophy**: Ivory (#FAF8F4) + Emerald (#1B5E3B) + Gold (#C8A951) + Charcoal (#2C2C2C) — clean, trustworthy, premium
- **Layout Paradigm**: Asymmetric hero with marble texture, full-width emerald band sections, floating gold-accented cards, off-center typography
- **Signature Elements**: Marble SVG texture backgrounds, thin gold divider lines, emerald corner accents on cards
- **Interaction Philosophy**: Smooth scroll, hover gold shimmer on buttons, card elevation on hover
- **Animation**: Fade-up on scroll entrance, gold shimmer on CTA, staggered card reveals
- **Typography System**: Playfair Display (headings) + Cormorant Garamond italic (taglines) + Source Sans 3 (body)
- **Probability**: 0.09
